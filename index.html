2nd District Court of Appeal Clerk Appointment

Online Booking Portal

 

Beginning July 12, 2021, the Clerk’s Office for the Second District Court of Appeal will be open to the public by appointment only.  Please see below for appointment instructions. 

 

In compliance with Los Angeles County Department of Public Health health and safety guidelines, the court has implemented steps to protect the staff and public while conducting court business.  Please adhere to the following procedures:

 

  • When visiting the court, a face mask or covering must be worn at all times in accordance with Los Angeles County Department of Public Health regardless of vaccination status.  This applies to attorneys, parties, court staff, vendors, and the general public.  The only exceptions to this policy will be for documented medical reasons or an ADA accommodation approved by the court.  Please note if an exception is granted, admittance into the court may be restricted to a specific time of the day or by scheduled appointment. 

 

  • The Clerk’s Office is inaccessible to the public unless a prescheduled reservation has been made. This policy applies to attorneys, parties, copying services, and justice partners for record review.  Due to limited capacity, reservations for a scanning stations for the public and Justice Partners are for 2 hours maximum and “See a Clerk” is 30 minutes maximum. 

 

  • Please confirm with a clerk prior to scheduling an appointment to ensure that the record is available.  For reservations, please call (213) 830-7000 or select the following: 

 

    • Clerk Office Appointments
      • See a Clerk – 30 minutes maximum
      • Scanning Station – 2 hours maximum
      • Justice Partners (AG, DA, and Public Defender) – 2 hours maximum
