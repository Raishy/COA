In compliance with Los Angeles County Department of Public Health health and safety guidelines, the court has implemented steps to protect the staff and public while conducting court business.  Please adhere to the following procedures:

 

  • When visiting the court, a face mask or covering must be worn at all times in accordance with Los Angeles County Department of Public Health.  This applies to attorneys, parties, court staff, vendors, and the general public.  The only exceptions to this policy will be for documented medical reasons or an ADA accommodation approved by the court.  Please note if an exception is granted, admittance into the court may be restricted to a specific time of the day or by scheduled appointment. 

 

  • The Clerk’s Office is inaccessible to the public unless a prescheduled reservation has been made. This policy applies to attorneys, parties, copying services, and justice partners for record review.  Due to limited capacity, reservations for a scanning station is for 2 hours maximum and “See a Clerk” is 30 minutes maximum. For reservations, please call (213) 830-7000 or select the following: 
    • Clerk Office Appointments
      • See a Clerk – 30 minutes maximum
      • Scanning Station – 2 hours maximum
      • Justice Partners’ – 2 hours maximum
